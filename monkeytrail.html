MONKEY TRAIL

Take a walk or ride through this challenging obstacle course.


The Monkey Trail is a 330 meter long (1,083 feet) path including steps, wooden boardwalk, and dirt trail that goes up over a steep incline. It rises about 30 meters in vertical elevation; The trail itself have dozens of monkeys. These are long-tailed macaque monkeys.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo.
